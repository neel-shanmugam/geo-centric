This website helps you find the closest locations of interest based on multiple individual addresses. Here's how to use it:

  • Enter your Google Maps API key.
  • Enter your place of interest (e.g., "fitness center").
  • Enter the address of each individual.
  • For each individual, you can choose whether or not to consider routes with tolls. Check the box if you want to consider routes with tolls.
  • You can add and remove individuals using the "Add Individual" and "Remove Individual" buttons.
  • When you're ready, click "Submit" to find the best locations.

Logging In

To view your past search history, you need to login. Click on the "Login" button on the homepage, and enter your credentials. If you do not have an account, you can register by clicking on the "Register" link and providing the required information.

Viewing Past Search History

After logging in, click on the "History" button in the navigation bar to see a list of your past searches. You can click on any search in the list to see the details of that search.
